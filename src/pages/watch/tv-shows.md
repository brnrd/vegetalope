A raw non-exhaustive list of TV shows my wife and I liked.

### Äkta människor

In a parallel present, the artificial human robot has become a fully integrated part of society.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2180271/) - Rating: 7.9

### Band of Brothers

The story of Easy Company of the U.S. Army 101st Airborne Division and their mission in World War II Europe.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0185906/) - Rating: 9.4

### Battlestar Galactica

When an old enemy, the Cylons, resurface and obliterate the 12 colonies, the crew of the aged Galactica protect a small civilian fleet in search of the fabled 13th colony, Earth.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0407362/) - Rating: 8.7

### Bewitched

A witch married to an ordinary man cannot resist using her magic powers to solve the problems her family faces.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0057733/) - Rating: 7.5

### Black Mirror

An anthology series exploring a twisted, high-tech multiverse where humanity's greatest innovations and darkest instincts collide.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2085059/) - Rating: 8.8

### Carnivàle

During the Great Depression, a mysterious creature known as the Management recruits a young man to join a traveling carnival.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0319969/) - Rating: 8.4

### Cruel Summer

A small Texas town when a beautiful popular teen, Kate, is abducted, and seemingly unrelated, a girl named Jeanette goes from being a sweet awkward outlier to the most popular girl in town, and by '95, the most despised person in America.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt11083696/) - Rating: 7.5

### Escape at Dannemora

An employee at a prison in upstate New York becomes romantically involved with a pair of inmates and helps them escape.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt6866266/) - Rating: 8.0

### Fargo

Various chronicles of deception, intrigue and murder in and around frozen Minnesota. Yet all of these tales mysteriously lead back one way or another to Fargo, North Dakota.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2802850/) - Rating: 8.9

### Foundation

A complex saga of humans scattered on planets throughout the galaxy all living under the rule of the Galactic Empire.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0804484/) - Rating: 7.4

### Freaks and Geeks

A high school mathlete starts hanging out with a group of burnouts while her younger brother navigates his freshman year.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0193676/) - Rating: 8.8

### Kleo

After the fall of the Berlin Wall, a former East German spy resolves to find out who betrayed her and why, using her skills as a highly trained assassin.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt11555848/) - Rating: 7.2

### Lost

The survivors of a plane crash are forced to work together in order to survive on a seemingly deserted tropical island.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0411008/) - Rating: 8.3

### Love On The Spectrum

A documentary series following young adults on the autism spectrum as they explore the unpredictable world of love, dating and relationships.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt11286496/) - Rating: 8.3

### Mad Men

A drama about one of New York's most prestigious ad agencies at the beginning of the 1960s, focusing on one of the firm's most mysterious but extremely talented ad executives, Donald Draper.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0804503/) - Rating: 8.6

### Misfits

A group of young offenders doing community service get struck by lightning during a storm, and begin to develop superpowers.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1548850/) - Rating: 8.2

### Sherlock

A modern update finds the famous sleuth and his doctor partner solving crime in 21st century London.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1475582/) - Rating: 9.1

### Squid Game

Hundreds of cash-strapped contestants accept an invitation to compete in children's games for a tempting prize, but the stakes are deadly.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt10919420/) - Rating: 8.0

### Stranger Things

When a young boy disappears, his mother, a police chief, and his friends must confront terrifying supernatural forces in order to get him back.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt4574334/) - Rating: 8.7

### The Bible

A religious drama miniseries chronicling various biblical stories from the Book of Genesis to Revelation.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2245988/) - Rating: 7.4

### The Crown

Follows the political rivalries and romance of Queen Elizabeth II's reign and the events that shaped the second half of the 20th century.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt4786824/) - Rating: 8.6

### The Dick Van Dyke Show

The misadventures of a TV writer both at work and at home.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0054533/) - Rating: 8.4

### The Leftovers

Three years after the disappearance of 2% of the global population, a group of people in a small New York community try to continue their lives while coping with the tragedy of the unexplained nature of the event.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2699128/) - Rating: 8.3

### The Mandalorian

The travels of a lone bounty hunter in the outer reaches of the galaxy, far from the authority of the New Republic.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt8111088/) - Rating: 8.7

### The OA

Having gone missing seven years ago, the previously blind Prairie returns home, now in her twenties with her sight restored. While many believe she is a miracle, others worry that she could be dangerous.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt4635282/) - Rating: 7.8

### The Prisoner (2009)

An update to the cult 1960s series, where a man is trapped in a mysterious and surreal village without knowing how he got there, and is constantly pursued by the enigmatic leader, Number Two.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1043714/) - Rating: 6.2

### The Twilight Zone

An updated version of the classic anthology series featuring various tales of science fiction, mystery, and horror.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2583620/) - Rating: 5.8

### The War at Home

A sitcom about the daily lives of a family with three children.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0460692/) - Rating: 6.9

### True Blood

Telepathic waitress Sookie Stackhouse encounters a strange new supernatural world when she meets the mysterious Bill Compton, a southern Louisiana gentleman and vampire.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0844441/) - Rating: 7.9

### Wonderfalls

A Niagara Falls souvenir shop clerk finds her life changing in unexpected ways when inanimate objects begin talking to her.

[IMDb](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0361256/) - Rating: 8.2
